Cube

There is value in our differences. Each difference is a new way to look at the world. The way that we learn to appreciate different ideologies is through simple conversation. CUBE is the start of a social movement of conversation geared towards getting people engaged with their community. The concept is built around the idea that alone we can only see one side of a problem/topic but together a “cube” or the whole problem.
